About Jesusify

Jesusify is a spiritual music platform designed to bring you closer to God through worship, sermons, podcasts, and scripturally inspired playlists. We aim to provide a clean, uplifting, and distraction-free environment for spiritual growth through music.

Version

Jesusify v1.0 – Launched with love and blessings in 2025

Team & Vision

Built by a passionate group of developers and worshipers. Our vision is to empower believers through music and make Jesus-centered content accessible to everyone, everywhere.
